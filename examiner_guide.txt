               FAKE NEWS DETECTION SYSTEM - TESTING GUIDE
======================================================================

Dear Examiner / Evaluator,

This project implements a local, GPU-accelerated Fake News Detector powered 
by a fine-tuned RoBERTa transformer model. The model has been trained and 
evaluated on the complete WELFake dataset containing 72,134 news articles, 
achieving a final accuracy of 97.42%.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTE ON INPUT STYLE:
The model operates as a stylistic and linguistic classifier. It evaluates 
the syntax, vocabulary, and writing style of the input to determine if it 
resembles official news reporting (AP, Reuters style) or unreliable/clickbait 
writing (social media, internet forum style).

Please enter inputs as formal headlines or news statements, rather than 
casual search engine questions. For example:
- YES: "Donald Trump is the president of the United States" (Statement)
- NO:  "is Trump the president of USA?" (Question)
